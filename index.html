Frequently Asked Questions


  • Will the bouncer be clean?
    • Spotless! We wash, sanitize, and dry after each rental
  • What surfaces do you set up on?
    • Grass, turf, concrete, pavers, asphalt, or indoors. NEVER on gravel or dirt
  • Do you require a deposit?
    • We require a $50 non-refundable deposit that goes toward the price of your rental to book.
  • How far do you travel?
    • We deliver anywhere in SE Idaho. Please message us if you are curious about a specific location!
  • What form of payment do you accept?
    • We accept debit and credit cards, as well as cash and Venmo. If paying in cash, please have the exact amount ready.

About Us!

Hi! I’m Presli Hastings, thank you so much for being here! My husband Brady and I were both born and raised in Southeast Idaho and we LOVE living here with our three kids.

About a year ago, I noticed a huge hole in the market for cute bounce houses. I decided to bring high-quality, beautiful inflatables to this area and Mountain Castle Events was born. We are fully licensed and insured and ready to party with you! We can’t wait to get to know you!
